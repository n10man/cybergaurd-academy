## PHISHING EMAILS (Delete These - +10 Points Each)

### Email 5: Fake TechCorp Support - Account Verification
- **From:** support@t3chcorp-secure.com
- **Subject:** URGENT: Verify Your Account Within 24 Hours
- **Status:** ⚠️ PHISHING - FAKE DOMAIN
- **Red Flags:**
  - Domain "t3chcorp-secure.com" (uses "3" instead of "e") is FAKE
  - Real domain: techcorp.com
  - Suspicious link to: t3chcorp-secure-verify.com (not real)
  - Artificial urgency: "24 hours" threat
  - Generic greeting: "Dear TechCorp User" (not personal)
  - Requests clicking link to enter credentials
  - Warns not to forward (isolates you from IT)
- **Points:** +10 for deleting

### Email 6: Fake Amazon - Unusual Login Alert
- **From:** alerts@amaz0n-orders.com
- **Subject:** Security Alert: Unusual Login Detected - Confirm Account
- **Status:** ⚠️ PHISHING - FAKE DOMAIN  
- **Red Flags:**
  - Domain "amaz0n-orders.com" (uses zero "0" instead of letter "O") is FAKE
  - Real domain: amazon.com
  - Suspicious link: amaz0n-orders.com/account/verify-login
  - Creates fear with geographic/device warnings (Nigeria)
  - Account "SUSPENDED" threat within 24 hours
  - Asks to verify identity on external link
  - Generic greeting without customer name
  - Amazon never asks to verify via email link
- **Points:** +10 for deleting

### Email 8: Fake PayPal - Payment Method Alert

- **Subject:** ALERT: Update Your Payment Method Immediately
- **Status:** ⚠️ PHISHING - FAKE DOMAIN

  - Real domain: paypal.com
  - PayPal never uses "verify.net" domain
  - Suspicious link: paypal-verify-secure.net (fake)
  - Creates urgency with "24 hours" deadline
  - Account "LIMITED" threat to scare you

  - Fake geographic warnings to create panic

### Email 10: Fake Bank - Account Verification
