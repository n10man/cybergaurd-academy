## Tips for Success

1. **Check the domain** - Is it really from the company?
2. **Look for urgency** - Phishing uses time pressure
3. **Read carefully** - Generic greetings are a red flag
4. **Don't trust links** - Visit company sites directly instead
5. **Think first** - Take time to analyze each email
